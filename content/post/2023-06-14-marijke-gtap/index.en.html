We are very proud that our colleague Marijke Kuiper was inducted into the GTAP Research Fellow Hall of Fame at the 26th Annual Conference on Global Economic Analysis that took place in Bordeaux, from 14 to 16 June. The GTAP Hall of fame lists all persons that provided significant and continuing contributions to the development of GTAP. Congrats Marijke!
